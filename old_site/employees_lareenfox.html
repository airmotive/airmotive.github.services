Lareen Fox
  Lareen (Bookie) Fox has been the secretary for Airmotive Service since 1982 when Mom Larsen retired.  Before that she answered the telephone for Wayne from her home switchboard.  A few years later she started flagging for Wayne while he did his aerial application.  Her primary responsibilities are the bookkeeping (hence, Bookie), scheduling of Wayne's time and appointments, answering the telephone, keeping the business machine working smoothly...and a little of just about everything else.
