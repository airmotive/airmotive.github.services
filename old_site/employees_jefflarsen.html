Jeff Larsen
 

  Jeffrey (Jeff) W. Larsen is Wayne's son.  He has an Associates Degree from Utah State University in Aeronautical Technology and has an Airframe & Power Plant Mechanics license.   He is a private pilot and spends a lot of his time at the airport doing airplane mechanics and aircraft log book paperwork.  He does well with electronic installations.
